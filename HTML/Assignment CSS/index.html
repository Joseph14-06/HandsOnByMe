About Us

Mangalam College of Engineering (MLM) stands supreme in the field of technical education with excellence and quality in education. In keeping with MLM's goal of excellence, the academic programs is supported by well experienced and dedicated faculty, which is committed to equip the student with knowledge and skills needed to succeed in today's rapidly changing world.
